Cybersecurity Policy Starter Pack — Checklist

Brand: Tachu Cyber GRC
Download Markdown

How to use this checklist

  • Owner: CISO / IT Lead / Compliance
  • Scope: Company‑wide or business unit
  • Maturity target: Baseline (2 wks) · Core (60 days) · Advanced (120+)

Quick‑Start Tiers

  • Baseline: essentials to reduce top risks quickly
  • Core: hardening, visibility, and response
  • Advanced: depth, automation, and assurance

1) Governance & Roles

  • Approve a Security Policy Framework and RACI
  • Risk acceptance & exception process
  • Policy repository with access to all staff

2) Risk & Assets

  • Asset‑based risk register; rating method
  • Review risks with leadership quarterly
  • Authoritative asset inventory

4) IAM & Account Security

  • Least privilege & RBAC; SSO for major apps
  • Lifecycle provisioning & quarterly access reviews
  • MFA coverage and password standards

6) Endpoint & Hardening

  • EDR, encryption, auto‑patching
  • Baseline hardening (CIS/vendor)
  • Config drift checks

8) Data Protection & Privacy

  • Classification; encryption in transit/at rest
  • DLP (start with alerts); retention

10) Backup & Recovery

  • 3‑2‑1 backups; quarterly restores
  • Protect backup admin with MFA & separation

13) Logging & Detection

  • Centralized logging; top 10 use‑cases
  • Time sync (NTP)

14) Incident Response

  • IR plan and on‑call escalation
  • Tabletop exercises; breach notification

15) BC/DR

  • Critical processes and tolerances
  • BC/DR plan and annual test

17) DevSecOps

  • Branch protection; secrets scanning
  • SAST/DAST/Dependency scanning in CI

Tip: Use this pack to drive a lightweight evidence set (policies, approvals, scans, training).
