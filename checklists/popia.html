POPIA Compliance — Starter Checklist

Brand: Tachu Cyber GRC · South Africa · POPIA
Download Markdown

How to use

  • Owner: Information Officer (IO) / Deputy IO / Legal-Privacy / Security
  • Scope: Company‑wide or business unit
  • Review cadence: Quarterly / Semi‑annual / Annual

Quick‑Start Tiers

  • Baseline (Day 0–30): Appoint/register IO, publish privacy notices, enable DSR & incident notification.
  • Core (Day 31–90): Data mapping, lawful bases & retention, operator contracts, direct marketing governance.
  • Advanced (Day 91–180): Prior‑authorisation, cross‑border controls, audits & KPIs.

1) Governance & Accountability

  • Appoint/register IO & Deputies with the Information Regulator
  • Approve Privacy Policy Framework; maintain POPIA control register
  • Integrate POPIA with PAIA Manual and access process

2) Data Mapping & Records

  • Maintain a Processing Register (data, purposes, recipients, storage, retention)
  • Identify special personal information & children’s data; operators & cross‑border transfers

3) Lawful Processing

  • Justification ground per purpose; consent capture/withdrawal records where used
  • Minimality; collect directly unless exception applies; objection enablement

4) Purpose & Retention

  • Specific purposes; privacy notices at/before collection
  • Retention & disposal schedules; destruction logs

8) Security Safeguards

  • Appropriate technical & organisational measures; operator agreements
  • Breach response: detect, assess, and notify Regulator/subjects when required

9) Data Subject Rights

  • Enable access, correction, deletion, objection with SLAs
  • Identity verification; secure delivery; DSR register & turnaround tracking

Tip: Align this POPIA checklist with your cybersecurity controls and evidence pack.
